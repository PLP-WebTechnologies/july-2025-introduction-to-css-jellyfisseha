General Fruit Information

Fruits provide essential vitamins and nutrients. Each fruit can be eaten fresh, juiced, or used in recipes.

Apple

Apples are great for snacks, pies, juices, and salads. Rich in fiber and vitamin C.

Banana

Bananas are perfect for smoothies, snacks, and baking. High in potassium and energy-boosting sugars.

Orange

Oranges are commonly juiced, eaten fresh, or added to desserts. Packed with vitamin C.

Strawberry

Strawberries are delicious in desserts, salads, and smoothies. Excellent source of antioxidants.

Grapes

Grapes are used for fresh consumption, juices, and wine. High in vitamins and natural sugars.

Mango

Mangoes are enjoyed fresh, in smoothies, or desserts. Known as the king of fruits, rich in vitamins A and C.

Pineapple

Pineapples are used in juices, desserts, and savory dishes. Contains bromelain, which aids digestion.

Watermelon

Watermelons are refreshing in summer, used in salads, juices, and desserts. Hydrating and low in calories.
